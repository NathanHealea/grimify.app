# Paint Selection & Hover

**Epic:** Color Wheel Visualization
**Type:** Feature
**Status:** Todo

## Summary

Allow users to interact with individual paint circles on the color wheel through hover and click actions. Hovering previews paint details; clicking selects a paint for persistent inspection.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Hover over a paint circle to preview its details in the side panel
- [ ] Click a paint to select it (persists until clicked again or another paint is selected)
- [ ] Selected paints show a dashed white ring
- [ ] Hovered paints glow
